Kore Business – Unified Retail & POS Portal

Kore Business is the central operational portal for business owners and staff to control storefront inventory, review orders, manage cash registers, and track daily shifts.

Core Capabilities

  • Point-of-Sale Register and Drawer Reconciliation
  • Real-time Multi-tenant Catalog & Stock Control
  • Storefront templates deployment configuration
  • Shift tracking & cashier access control
